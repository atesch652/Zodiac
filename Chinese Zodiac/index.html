Chinese Zodiac

It is believed that the The Jade Emperor (The Emperor in Heaven in Chinese folklore) called for a race amongst the animals in order to determine the assignment of the 12-year cycle. Each animal used what advantage they had in order to finish the race in time. The order they finished determined the order of the cycle. This 12-year cycle is determined using the Chinese lunar calender which varies from the western calender we are used to!
